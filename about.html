Hi! I'm Andre,
A UX/UI Designer from Melbourne, Australia.

The visual image and design has been a continual passion of mine. Importantly I love how creative design can improve the lives of people and society.

My design/creative journey began in Italy before moving to Scotland, where I gained a BA Honours in Drawing and Painting. Immersing myself in the cultures of both countries taught me the value of different perspectives.

When I am not at the computer or behind the camera, I am spending time with my family. When I am not with my family I enjoy indulging my other passions such as Hiking, Camping, Painting, Drawing, Martial Arts, and being a tragic AFL fan.
